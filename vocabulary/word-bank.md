## Words Learned

*(Newest at the top)*

**Keep the change** — tell a cashier to keep extra money — "Here's 100 baht. Keep the change."
**Here you go** — a phrase when handing something to someone — "Here you go, your order is ready."
**I'll take this one** — phrase to say you want to buy something — "I'll take this one, please."
**Do you have...?** — ask if a shop sells something — "Do you have mango sticky rice?"
**How much is this?** — ask the price of something — "How much is this shirt?"
**Can I have...?** — politely order or request something — "Can I have an Americano, please?"
**on weekdays** — Monday to Friday — "On weekdays I work from home."
**o'clock** — exactly on the hour — "The class starts at 3 o'clock."
**PM** — after noon (12:00–23:59) — "Lunch is at 12 PM."
**AM** — before noon (00:00–11:59) — "I wake up at 6 AM."
**midnight** — 12 AM, the middle of the night — "I go to bed before midnight."
**noon** — 12 PM, the middle of the day — "I have lunch at noon."
**daily routine** — the things you do every day — "My daily routine starts at 6 AM."
**skip (a meal)** — to not eat a meal — "I skip breakfast on busy days."
**flexible hours** — a work schedule where you choose your start/end time — "My office has flexible hours."
**go to bed** — to start sleeping for the night — "I go to bed at 10 PM."
**wake up** — to get out of bed in the morning — "I wake up at 6 AM."
**What do you do?** — a question to ask about someone's job or hobby — "What do you do?" / "I play video games."
**Where are you from?** — a question to ask someone's country — "Where are you from?" / "I'm from Thailand."
**What's your name?** — a question to ask someone's name — "Hi! What's your name?"
**Nice to meet you too** — the reply when someone says "Nice to meet you" — "Nice to meet you too!"
**Nice to meet you** — a phrase to greet someone new — "Hi! I'm Alex. Nice to meet you!"
