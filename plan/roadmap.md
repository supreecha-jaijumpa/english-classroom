# My English Learning Roadmap

## Overall Path: A1 → A2 → B1 → B2 → C1

---

## Stage 1: A1 — Absolute Beginner (Months 1–2)
**Goal:** Survive basic daily situations in English

### Topics to Cover
- [x] Greetings and introductions ("Hi, my name is...")
- [ ] Numbers 1–100
- [x] Days of the week and months
- [ ] Colors, shapes, sizes
- [ ] Family members (mother, father, sister...)
- [ ] Common verbs: be, have, like, want, go
- [ ] Yes/No questions ("Are you...?", "Do you...?")
- [ ] Basic sentence: Subject + Verb + Object

### Milestone Test
> Can I introduce myself, ask someone's name, and say basic facts about my life?

---

## Stage 2: A2 — Elementary (Months 3–4)
**Goal:** Talk about daily life and routine

### Topics to Cover
- [ ] Daily routines (I wake up, I eat, I go to work...)
- [ ] Time expressions (in the morning, at 3pm, every day)
- [ ] Present simple tense
- [x] Past simple tense (I went, I ate, I saw...) ← covered in Session 7
- [ ] Shopping and prices
- [ ] Food and ordering at a restaurant
- [x] Directions (turn left, go straight, next to...) ← covered in Session 5
- [x] Feelings and emotions ← covered in Session 6

### Milestone Test
> Can I describe my typical day and have a short conversation about food or shopping?

---

## Stage 3: B1 — Pre-Intermediate (Months 5–6+)
**Goal:** Hold a real conversation on familiar topics

### Topics to Cover
- [ ] Future plans (I will..., I'm going to...)
- [ ] Talking about experiences (present perfect: I have been...)
- [ ] Opinions and preferences ("I think...", "I prefer...")
- [ ] Making phone calls and sending messages
- [ ] Travel and transportation
- [ ] Work and daily responsibilities
- [ ] Telling stories (past tenses: simple + continuous)

### Milestone Test
> Can I have a 5-minute conversation with a native speaker about my life?

---

## Stage 4: B2 — Upper Intermediate (Months 7–10)
**Goal:** Speak and write clearly on a wide range of topics

### Topics to Cover
- [ ] Complex tenses (past perfect, conditionals: if I had...)
- [ ] Passive voice (It was built, It is used...)
- [ ] Formal and informal writing (emails, messages)
- [ ] Expressing opinions with reasons ("I believe... because...")
- [ ] Debating and disagreeing politely
- [ ] News, media, and current events
- [ ] Idiomatic expressions (break a leg, under the weather...)
- [ ] Academic and work vocabulary

### Milestone Test
> Can I write a clear email, understand a news article, and debate a topic comfortably?

---

## Stage 5: C1 — Advanced (Months 11–14+)
**Goal:** Express ideas fluently, precisely, and with nuance

### Topics to Cover
- [ ] Advanced grammar (subjunctive, inversion, cleft sentences)
- [ ] Academic writing (essays, reports, summaries)
- [ ] Nuance and tone (formal, informal, sarcastic, diplomatic)
- [ ] Complex vocabulary and collocations
- [ ] Understanding humor, idioms, and cultural references
- [ ] Listening to native speakers at full speed (podcasts, films)
- [ ] Speaking with confidence on unfamiliar topics
- [ ] Professional communication (meetings, presentations)

### Milestone Test
> Can I express complex ideas clearly, write academic-level text, and hold any conversation with a native speaker?
